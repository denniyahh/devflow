<objective>
20a — `VersionBump` must rewrite workspace member self-pins.

`version::write_version` rewrites exactly one dotted field
(`field_for()` returns `workspace.package.version` for a workspace `Cargo.toml`).
A published Cargo workspace states its version twice: once in
`[workspace.package] version` and again as an explicit `version` pin on every
`[workspace.dependencies]` entry that points at a workspace member by `path`.
Nothing rewrites the second one, so the self-pin ships stale and `cargo publish`
rejects the upload as a duplicate on release day — shipped broken two for two
(v1.5.0 patched by `7ad260c`, v1.6.0 by PR #15).

Purpose: make DevFlow's own (and any user's) published-workspace release cut
correct by construction; turn the existing PR #17 guard from a manual tripwire
into a no-op-by-construction.
Output: an additive inline-table rewrite pass in `version.rs`; the guard test
green without manual edits.

Root cause (verified at HEAD): `find_version_in_contents` (version.rs:226) and
`replace_version_in_contents` (version.rs:253) both `continue` past values that
`starts_with('{')` — inline tables — which is exactly why `[workspace.dependencies]`
self-pins are invisible today. The fix is an ADDITIVE pass over inline-table
entries with a local `path`, NOT a modification of that guard (single-field
callers still need it).

**Path note:** `version.rs` lives at `crates/devflow-core/src/version.rs`, NOT
`devflow-cli` — CONTEXT.md's canonical-refs originally said `cli`; corrected
during research (Pitfall 1).
</objective>
