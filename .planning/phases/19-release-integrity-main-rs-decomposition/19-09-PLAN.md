<objective>
Finish the split: move the remaining two clusters out of `main.rs` and reduce it
to a thin crate root.

`commands.rs` is the largest cluster by line count (~1,280 production lines
before `start` joins it) but the least coupled — `19-RESEARCH.md`'s
responsibility map records it as self-contained. `parallel.rs` is small and
independent. Neither presents the coupling that made 19-08 the hard plan.

whether the four env-parsing helpers warrant their own file. **They do** — see
Task 3's rationale.

Purpose: the phase's stated goal is that later phases stop paying the
near-serial wave tax. That only happens once `main.rs` is small enough that a
Phase 20 plan touching `--until` and a plan touching `gate show` do not collide.
Output: `parallel.rs`, `commands.rs`, `config_parse.rs`, and a thin `main.rs`.
